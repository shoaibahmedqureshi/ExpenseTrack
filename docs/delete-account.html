Delete Your Outlay Account

You can request deletion of your Outlay account and all associated data at any time.

This action is permanent. Once deleted, your account, all expense records, budgets, and profile data cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All expense and income records
  • All budgets
  • Your profile (name, currency preference, avatar)

How to request deletion:

Send an email to 08bitsaqureshi@seecs.edu.pk with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your Outlay account. We will delete your account and all data within 30 days and confirm by email.
